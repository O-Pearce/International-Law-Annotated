What is this?

We’re building a clean, plain English layer of commentary on top of international legal texts. Each case page mirrors the original structure and allows readers to hover on paragraph numbers to see brief annotations — doctrine, context, and critiques — authored by a small, global network of early-career public international lawyers.

Our goals are clarity, accessibility, and scholarly usefulness. This is a community project: annotations are concise, sourced, and signed by their authors, but should not be regarded as thoroughly peer-reviewed.
